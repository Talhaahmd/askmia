Everything You Need to Know About Ask MIA

What is Ask MIA and how does it work?

Ask MIA (Multilingual Interactive Agent) is an AI-powered assistant that chats, translates, and automates customer interactions across languages. It understands voice or text messages, processes them through natural-language AI, and delivers accurate, human-like replies instantly.

Can MIA integrate with my existing tools or CRM?

Yes! MIA connects seamlessly with Make.com, Supabase, Stripe, and WhatsApp Business API. You can automate bookings, send payment links, or sync messages directly to your CRM — no coding required.

Does Ask MIA support voice messages?

Absolutely. Users can send voice notes in any language, and MIA transcribes, translates, and replies in real time. It’s perfect for multilingual support through WhatsApp or your web chat.

Which languages can MIA understand?

MIA supports over 40 languages, including English, Urdu, Arabic, French, Spanish, and Turkish. It automatically detects the user’s language and responds naturally without manual switching.

Is my data secure with Ask MIA?

Security is our top priority. All chats and user data are encrypted end-to-end, stored securely, and compliant with GDPR and regional data-protection laws. Only you control your data.
